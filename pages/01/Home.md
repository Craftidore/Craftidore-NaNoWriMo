## What is 'Right Place, Right Time?'

Originally meant to be my story for NaNoWriMo 2021, *Right Place, Right Time* is a science fiction story about time travel.
Unfortunately I didn't complete NaNoWriMo 2021 within November, and I decided to take a step back, plan my story out better, and try again.
This would be try \#2.
